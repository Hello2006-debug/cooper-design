Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers!

We offer comprehensive interior design services including residential design, commercial spaces, design consultation, space planning, renovation projects, furniture selection, color consulting, and full project management from concept to completion.

Our pricing varies based on project scope, size, and complexity. We offer both hourly rates and flat-fee packages. Initial consultations are complimentary, and we'll provide a detailed quote after understanding your specific needs. Projects typically range from $5,000 to $100,000+.

Timeline depends on project scope. A single room design typically takes 4-6 weeks, while full home designs can take 3-6 months. Renovation projects may take longer depending on construction requirements. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

While your input is valuable, you don't need to be present for every step. We'll schedule key meetings for concept presentations, material selections, and final walkthroughs. We can work remotely and communicate via email, phone, and video calls for your convenience.

We're based in Davidson, NC, and primarily serve the greater Charlotte area including Cornelius, Huntersville, Mooresville, and surrounding communities. We also take on select projects throughout North Carolina and neighboring states. Virtual design services are available nationwide.
